BMW

BMW stands for Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, which is a German multinational company that produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Established in 1916, BMW is renowned for its high-quality engineering, performance-oriented vehicles, and innovative technology. It is one of the leading manufacturers in the automotive industry, with a reputation for producing luxury cars, sporty vehicles, and motorcycles that offer a blend of performance, comfort, and style.
